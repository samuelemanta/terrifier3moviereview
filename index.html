Terrifier 3 - Movie Review

Introduction

Terrifier 3 was my first horror movie experience, even though it falls under the slasher and splatter horror genre rather than pure horror. The movie is packed with bloodbaths rather than typical scary scenes, but that’s exactly what makes it a unique part of the horror genre.

Why I Watched Terrifier 3

I was initially drawn to this movie because of a glowing review from my favorite movie critic, Victorlazlo88. He described Terrifier 3 as a “gold” movie in today’s era, and praised it for its impressive box office results, unique style, and iconic character Art the Clown. He even compared Art to classic horror icons like Leatherface, calling it an extreme, violent film rare in the mainstream.

First Viewing

My first time watching Terrifier 3 was tough, given its intense and unexpected gore. However, I quickly noticed elements of cinematic mastery, especially in certain scenes and musical choices.

The shots of Sienna Shaw's new bedroom were especially stunning. Despite its graphic violence, I found myself appreciating the movie more and more.

Second Viewing

On my second viewing, I was able to enjoy the movie even more. Knowing what was coming allowed me to focus on the details, like Art the Clown's facial expressions, movements, and dark humor. His character is simply unforgettable and his performance is perfect.

Storyline & Connection to Other Films

After watching Terrifier 3, I decided I needed to watch Terrifier 2 to better understand the story of the "final girl," Sienna Shaw. Her character fascinated me, and I’m eager to see how her story unfolds.

Pros and Cons

  • Pros:
    • Visually stunning shots, especially of Sienna’s bedroom
    • Unique soundtrack
    • Dark humor and perfect facial expressions by Art the Clown
  • Cons:
    • Over-the-top jokes (only two that I disliked)

Similar Movies to Watch

  1. Terrifier (2016) and Terrifier 2 (2022)
  2. Hatchet (2006)
  3. The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (1974)
  4. Halloween (1978) and Halloween (2018)
  5. Mandy (2018)

Conclusion

Despite its intense scenes, Terrifier 3 has become one of my favorite movies. It truly feels like "my movie," one I could watch over and over. This movie is unique, unsettling, and darkly comedic, and I’m grateful I decided to watch it.
